VastraDaan (Clothes Donation)

Why Clothes Matter?

For many underprivileged families, buying new clothes is a luxury they cannot afford. VastraDaan aims to bridge this gap by collecting usable clothes and distributing them to those in need.

Program Goal: To provide dignity and warmth to 10,000+ individuals this year.

How You Can Contribute

Collect Old Clothes

Gather gently used clothes from your home or neighborhood.

Drop at Center

Visit our nearest drop-off point or request a pickup.
