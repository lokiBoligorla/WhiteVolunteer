School Projects

We are dedicated to improving the quality of education and life for school children. Our projects range from renovating classrooms to providing essential learning materials.

Empowering Through Education

At White Volunteers Foundation, we believe that education is the most powerful weapon to change the world. Our school projects are designed to create a conducive learning environment for students in underprivileged areas.

Educational Materials

Distributing textbooks, notebooks, and stationery to ensure every child has the tools to learn.

Infrastructure Support

Renovating classrooms, painting walls, and fixing furniture to make schools a vibrant place.

Health Camps

Organizing regular health checkups and hygiene awareness camps for students.
